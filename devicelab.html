What is the Madworks Device Lab?

In short, it's a place where a variety of internet connected devices (smartphones, tablets, computers) are kept together as a testing ground for web sites and mobile apps.

Open Device Laps (ODLs) are a grass roots community movement that establish shared pools of devices for use by web and app developers, since the cost of owning so many devides is prohibitive for small firms, startups, and independent developers.

Madworks Device Lab is the ninth ODL in the United States, and the first and only ODL in Wisconsin. More on ODLs at: opendevicelab.com

Open Device Labs:

  • Are an opportunity for device manufactures and retails to contribute to and build relationships with a key demographic of their users
  • Provide developers with a convenient way to test their products on multiple devices at once
  • Improve the quality and security of web and app development
  • Are a fertile ground for collaboration and innovation

What does it cost to use?

Madworks Device Lab is FREE. ODLs are meant to be a non-profit and free for the community to use. Through partnerships, donations, and volunteers, we hope to keep it that way!

How can I support Open Device Labs?

  • Temporarily or permanently donate devices you can spare
  • Donate other goods (ask what the lab needs most)
  • Spread the word!

Want to sponser or contribute?

Are you a manufacturer or other business? You can promote your product to developers by contributing to an ODL

Madworks Device lab helps local developers optimize content for consumption on YOUR devices, effectively optimizing the experience for YOUR customers!
