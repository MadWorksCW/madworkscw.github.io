madworks@CAMPUS

Located at 1401 University Avenue (one block west of Wisconsin Institutes for Discovery), we offer entrepreneurs the opportunity to participate in a summer accelerator program that can provide up to $10,000 in funding as you and your team seek commercial viability for your endeavor.

Our summer accelerator is based on best practices of lean start-up. If selected you and your team will work with our staff and mentors in seeking verification of the commercial viability of your business model. We will work with you in setting milestones which will provide access to additional capital for your efforts. At summers end we will seek an additional round of funding.
