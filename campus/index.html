madworks at UW CAMPUS

A Summer Accelerator for Entrepreneurs

Located at 1401 University Avenue (one block west of the Wisconsin Institutes for Discovery), Madworks at UW Campus provides a wide range of services to entrepreneurs from the University of Wisconsin and the general public. In the short term, Madworks will offer entrepreneurs the opportunity to participate in a summer accelerator program that can provide up to $10,000 in funding and $15,000 in support services to start up companies seeking commercial viability for their endeavor. There is no limitation on the market focus of companies looking to be part of this summer accelerator. Applicants with background in social media, life sciences, medical software, engineering, fine arts, humanities, business, manufacturing and other industries are encouraged to apply.

Application Process

All applications will be screened and ranked by a committee experienced in technology evaluation. There will be a bias towards companies that can present a clear vision of how they will reach commercialization of their product(s). The summer accelerator will be based on best practices of lean start up methodologies. Companies admitted in to the program will work with Madworks staff and mentors in seeking verification of the commercial viability of their business model. Part of the objectives for the summer will be to set milestones that when achieved will result in funding for each company. At the end of the summer all of the accelerator companies will be introduced to potential investors. Entrepreneurs interested in participating in the 2014 Summer Madworks accelerator class need to submit the electronic application found below by no later than May 28th.

Applications are no longer being accepted.

Follow up

After the initial summer accelerator class, Madworks at UW Campus will provide additional services during the 2014-15 school year. Along with a follow up accelerator program in the fall, Madworks will also have drop in mentoring and co-working space opportunities to entrepreneurs interested in starting and/or expanding their business ideas. Madworks has a staff that is experienced in helping start up companies and also has an extensive list of mentors and industry experts who can provide assistance on specific issues. Although Madworks will focus on assisting professors, staff, and students due to its proximity to the UW-Madison campus, the services provided by Madworks will be available to any entrepreneur who is willing to dedicate their passion and focus to making their company successful.
